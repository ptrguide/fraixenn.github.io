About pwf2tools

pwf2tools is a GUI and any-CPU build of ptr2tools, which was created by posesix and updated by RMGRich, which fixed many issues present in the OG version. The most noticeable difference with me and Rich's version is that mine is compatible with 32-bit computers, 
unlike ptr2tools, which is 64-bit only.
